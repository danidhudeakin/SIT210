The system was put through three phases. To test the BH1750 sensor, it was covered first and
light was illuminated on it to view the change in the lux value. Second, the PIR sensor was
individually tested to ensure that it was capable of detecting motion in the right way. Lastly,
the entire system was tested by integrating all the parts. Both LEDs switched on when the environment
was dark and motion was sensed and the Serial Monitor displayed the appropriate message. Motion was
observed but no change in LEDs occurred when the environment was bright. The slider switch was also
tested as an alternative input and it was able to switch both LEDs in spite of the light condition.
These tests ensured that the interrupt-based design and backup mechanism was working as it should.
